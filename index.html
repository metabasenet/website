What we build?

Game =>

To experience the prototype of the Metaverse in the simplest way with the Game. It can circulate freely between the systems by Game Props item certification on the blockchain, and can be realized or exchanged for items of equivalent value on other platforms . This allows the game to have The value attribute of the Metaverse.

<= NFT

The NFT use for unique On-Chain certified assets with traceability and certification capabilities. To prove the ownership of assets through NFT no longer requires complicated certification process, only need to trace the asset transfer trajectory in the blockchain, and to whom does the asset belong? It’s clear at a glance.

Shopping =>

In Metaverse, you can enjoy a visible Shopping experience. Not only can the items be seen, the transaction process can be seen, the traceability of the items can be seen, and even the production process can be seen. Of course, you can initiate arbitration at any time if you are not satisfied with the product. To punish the seller, the ultimate goal is that everything related to the product is transparent to you.

<= Sports

Enjoy a pleasant virtual sports time with friends by Metaverse Sports. Even if you stay at home, by wearable devices you can also exercise with your friends in the Metaverse. You could be completely immersed in sports during the experience, imagine that with your friends traveled through the Alps for an afternoon together on a stormy night, enjoying the beautiful Alpine afternoon sunshine.

Meeting =>

Even if you are thousands of mils away, you can still attend the company’s weekly Meetings on time. You can show everyone a perfect image without even wearing a formal dress, and you can participate in any meeting at any location anytime, anywhere.

<= Education

The Metaverse makes Education more vivid. In natural science courses, you can even see animals running and plants growing. You can even visit the planets in person when explaining the solar system or the universe. This must be a enjoy the process of Education.

Socialize =>

When we are still sharing text, pictures and videos, Metaverse will bring a new way of Socializing. Have you ever experienced a real feeling from the sharer? Sunshine, temperature, wind, rain, ground, and even pain, how beautiful the scenery is, how exciting it is to skydiving, these we will all become part of the Metaverse society.

 

What we have?

Para-Chain Support

Para-Chain can make different Metaverse applications not interfere with each other. All applications have excellent performance.

The creation of Para-Chain is also very simple. You only need to mortgage a certain amount of Token on the main network to create it, and you can even inherit from other networks.

Of course, you can also start from scratch. It all depends on you, and your Para-Chain is also as fast as other chains.

Smart Devices

Metaverse data is realized by a large number of sensors. Smart devices are indispensable. Of course, it may also be pure IoT sensors. It depends on different scenarios. For example, Metaverse Agri requires a large number of pure sensors, the Game scene requires the connection of many wearable smart devices. Then these IoT-related development platforms must be:

  • Arduino [View Documents] 
    1 Arduino is one of the best-known names in open-source IoT projects, encompassing both hardware and software.
2 The Arduino Cloud IoT is a cloud solution to configure, program, and connect IoT devices using the Arduino IoT Cloud service. 
3 The Arduino software includes two types of integrated development environments (IDE1 and IDE2). 
4 A variety of boards, shields, and carriers make up the hardware palette.
  • OpenRemote [View Documents] 
    1 Integration of different asset types.
2 support of standard protocols such as HTTP or MQTT as well as specific protocols like KNX for connecting IoT devices.
3 A customizable manager interface for the automation, monitoring, and control of processes, apps, and devices.
4 A mobile app so that you can connect to your phone services and push notifications,
5 Multi-tenancy with user roles and a full account management service.
  • ThingsBoard [View Documents] 
    1 IoT device management with monitoring and control mechanisms.
2 Scalability with the capability to orchestrate multiple devices at the same time, across the entire IoT ecosystem.
3 Enables users to create and manage alerts for connected devices (e.g. in the event of a disconnect or inactivity), other assets, and customers with real-time alarm monitoring. 
4 Extending default functionality with customizable rule chains, widgets, and transport implementations.
  • Thinger [View Documents] 
    1 A scalable IoT cloud platform for connecting devices.
2 Easy-to-navigate ready-to-use cloud infrastructure that allows users to integrate, monitor, and control millions of IoT devices.
3 Connect with any device, the most typical being Arduino, ESP8266, Raspberry Pi, and Intel Edison.
  • MainFlux [View Documents] 
    1 Device management.
2 Data aggregation & data management.
